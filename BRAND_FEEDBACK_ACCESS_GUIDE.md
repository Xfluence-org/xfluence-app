# How to Access Back-and-Forth Feedback on Brand/Agency Side

## Step-by-Step Guide:

### 1. Login as Brand/Agency User

### 2. Navigate to Campaigns
- Click on "Campaigns" in the sidebar
- URL: `/brand/campaigns`

### 3. Open a Campaign
- Click on any campaign card to open the Campaign Details Modal

### 4. Go to the "Influencers" Tab
- In the Campaign Details Modal, click on the "Influencers" tab
- You'll see sections for:
  - Active Influencers (those working on content)
  - Waiting Participants (those waiting for requirements)

### 5. Find Active Influencers
- Look for influencers in the "Active Influencers" section
- These are influencers who have received content requirements

### 6. Click "View Tasks" Button
- Each active influencer has two buttons: "View Progress" and "View Tasks"
- Click on **"View Tasks"**

### 7. View Tasks Modal Opens
- You'll see a list of tasks assigned to that influencer
- Each task shows:
  - Task title
  - Status badge
  - Progress bar
  - Number of feedback messages (if any)

### 8. Click on a Task
- Tasks are now clickable (hover to see the effect)
- Click on any task to open the Task Detail Modal

### 9. Task Detail Modal Shows Feedback
- The Task Detail Modal will open with the TaskWorkflowManager
- You'll see three tabs:
  - **Content Requirements** - Shows requirements AND feedback conversation
  - **Content Review** - For reviewing uploaded content
  - **Publish & Analytics** - For tracking published content

### 10. View the Conversation
- In the "Content Requirements" tab, scroll down to see:
  - The requirements you shared (marked with "Content Requirements" badge)
  - Feedback from the influencer
  - Your responses
  - All messages in chronological order with timestamps

## Visual Indicators:
- **Your messages**: Appear on the right with teal background
- **Influencer messages**: Appear on the left with gray background
- **Avatars**: Building icon for Brand, User icon for Influencer
- **Timestamps**: Show when each message was sent

## Quick Tip:
The feedback conversation includes both the content requirements you shared AND all back-and-forth messages, making it easy to track the entire communication history in one place.
